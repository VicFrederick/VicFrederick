Magic The Gathering

One of my long term hobbies is the trading card game, Magic te Gathering. Magic comes out with about 800 new cards every year, so there is a lot of space for creativity in the 60 cards I choose to play on any given Friday night. A few times a year in Texas there are also large tournaments. One of my goals is traveling to play in more large tourmanents.

Pioneer -A nonroatating format starting from the Retrun to Ravnica block with a banlist

This deck has a lot in common with modern GB Rock decks, but the top in goes significantly higher. Seasons Past and Dark Petition gives us the ability to go over most other midrange decks. The removal in this deck may need to move around as the metagame is established. The most difficult matchups for this deck is mono black agro in my expirance. Better blockers such as Courser of Kruphix or Sylvan Advocate would go a long way.
