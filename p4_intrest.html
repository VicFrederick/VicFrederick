Magic The Gathering

One of my long term hobbies is this trading card game. Magic comes out with about 800 new cards every year, so there is a lot of space for creativity in the 60 cards I choose to play on any given Friday night. A few times a year in Texas there are also large tournaments that are starting to aproach 1500 particapents. One of my goals is traveling to play in more large tourmanents.
